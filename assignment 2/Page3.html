Iron Man 2

(2010)

Genre: Action, Sci-Fi, Superhero

Synopsis:

Tony Stark, a genius billionaire and playboy, is kidnapped by terrorists while showcasing his company's latest weaponry in Afghanistan. Forced to build a weapon of mass destruction for his captors, he instead creates a high-tech suit of armor to escape. Upon returning home, Stark refines his suit and adopts the superhero persona of Iron Man. As he uncovers the sinister plans of his business partner, Obadiah Stane, he must use his newfound powers to stop Stane and protect the world from the misuse of his inventions. With heart, innovation, and a little humor, Iron Man marks the birth of a new era in superhero cinema.
